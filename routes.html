Suggested Travel Routes

Follow these carefully curated itineraries to see the best of BTS's Korea in a limited time.

1 Day BTS Seoul Tour

A fast-paced tour covering the most iconic spots in the capital city.

1

HYBE Headquarters: Start your morning at the home of BTS in Yongsan.

2

Seoul Forest: Take a peaceful walk in the park where members often visit.

3

Hongdae Area: Explore the vibrant neighborhood filled with BTS-themed cafes.

4

Banpo Hangang Park: End your day with a beautiful sunset at the Han River.

2 Day BTS Ultimate Tour

Day 1: Seoul BTS Locations

Visit HYBE, Seoul Forest, and various filming locations around Seoul.

Day 2: Gangneung Filming Locations

Take a KTX train to the East Coast to visit the famous BTS Bus Stop and Hyangho Beach.
