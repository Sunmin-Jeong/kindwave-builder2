Transport Guide

Getting around Korea is easy with its world-class public transportation system.

1. Seoul Subway

The Seoul subway system is the most efficient way to travel between BTS spots in the city. Most locations are within walking distance from subway stations.

  • Line 4 (Sinyongsan): For HYBE Headquarters.
  • Bundang Line (Seoul Forest): For Seoul Forest Park.
  • Line 3/7/9 (Express Bus Terminal): For Banpo Hangang Park.

2. T-Money Card

Buy a T-Money card at any convenience store (GS25, CU, 7-Eleven). You can use it for subways, buses, and even some taxis. You can often find limited edition BTS-themed T-Money cards!

3. Traveling to Gangneung

To visit the BTS Bus Stop, take the KTX (Korea Train Express) from Seoul Station to Gangneung Station. It takes about 2 hours. From there, you can take a local bus or a taxi to Hyangho Beach.
